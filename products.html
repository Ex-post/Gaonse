Frequently Asked Questions

How are your spices different from store-bought ones?

Our spices are hand-ground using traditional stone grinders which preserves their essential oils, aroma, and flavor. Unlike mass-produced spices that may contain additives and fillers, ours are 100% pure and processed in small batches to ensure freshness.

What makes cold-pressed mustard oil better?

Cold-pressed mustard oil is extracted without using heat, which preserves its natural nutrients, flavor, and medicinal properties. It retains the characteristic pungency and health benefits that are often lost in refined oils processed at high temperatures.

How should I store your products?

Our products should be stored in airtight containers in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Refrigeration is not necessary but can help extend the shelf life of ground spices and wheat flour. Cold-pressed mustard oil can be stored at room temperature.

Do you ship your products nationwide?

Currently, we serve customers in select cities across India. Please contact us for specific information about shipping to your location.

Are your products certified organic?

While our products follow natural farming practices and are free from chemical additives, we are in the process of obtaining organic certification. We work closely with farmers who follow sustainable agriculture practices and do not use synthetic pesticides or fertilizers.
