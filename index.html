Our Story

Pratap Enterprises was founded in 2013 in Munger, Bihar with a mission to bring quality, natural food products to urban homes. In 2023, we launched Gaon Se as a sub-brand that celebrates the rich agricultural traditions of Indian villages.

Our journey began with a simple idea: to bridge the gap between rural producers and urban consumers, enabling access to authentic, traditional foods while supporting village communities.
