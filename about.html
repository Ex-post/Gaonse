About Us

Discover the story behind Gaon Se and our mission to bring authentic, village-sourced products to your kitchen.

Our Story

Pratap Enterprises was founded in 2013 in Munger, Bihar, with a vision to connect rural producers with urban consumers. We specialize in delivering high-quality daily essentials, focusing on purity, traditional production methods, and building customer trust.

In 2023, we launched Gaon Se as a sub-brand that brings natural, village-sourced food products to urban homes. The name "Gaon Se" literally translates to "From the Village" - reflecting our commitment to authenticity and rural origins.

Our journey began when our founder visited his ancestral village and witnessed the decline of traditional food processing methods. He realized that modern, factory-produced food products couldn't match the flavor, nutrition, and purity of those made using traditional techniques in villages.

Today, we work directly with farmers and village artisans across Bihar to bring you products that are not only delicious but also support rural livelihoods and preserve traditional knowledge.

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between rural producers and urban consumers, bringing authentic, natural food products directly from Indian villages to urban kitchens while supporting rural livelihoods.

Our Vision

To create a sustainable ecosystem where traditional food processing methods thrive, rural communities prosper, and urban consumers enjoy the authentic taste and health benefits of village-sourced products.

Our Values

Authenticity, transparency, sustainability, rural empowerment, and preserving traditional knowledge are the core values that guide everything we do at Gaon Se.

Our Process

Sourcing

We carefully select farmers who follow natural farming practices, ensuring that our raw materials are of the highest quality and free from chemicals.

Processing

Our products are processed using traditional methods such as hand-grinding spices and cold-pressing oils, preserving their natural flavor, aroma, and nutritional value.

Quality Control

Every batch undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure purity, consistency, and adherence to our high standards before being packaged and shipped.

Delivery

From our village partners directly to your kitchen, ensuring that you receive products that are as fresh and authentic as possible.
